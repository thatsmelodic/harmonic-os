# Season Engine

## Purpose

The Season Engine lets Harmonic OS change mood, colors, featured content, and visual energy based on seasons, clothing drops, campaigns, or special events.

This matters because 2 Harmonic collections and creator content will evolve over time.

## Seasons

Default seasons:

- Fall.
- Winter.
- Spring.
- Summer.
- Special Drop.
- Launch Mode.
- Investor Mode.

## What A Season Controls

Each season can control:

- Primary color.
- Accent color.
- Glow color.
- Background effect.
- Hero headline.
- Featured world.
- Featured product/content.
- Animation mood.
- CTA copy.
- Music/sound mood.

## Example: Fall Season

Mood:

- Warm but still purple-led.
- Hoodie/zip-up focus.
- Deeper shadows.
- Burnt orange or red accent used sparingly.
- More texture.

Use case:

- Fall 2 Harmonic drop.
- Hoodies and zip-ups.
- Campaign countdown.

## Build Strategy

In early versions, seasons can be JSON/content configuration.

Later, the Control Hub should let the owner activate a season with a toggle.
