# Analytics System

## Purpose

Analytics shows what is working across Harmonic OS.

The goal is not just traffic numbers. The goal is business intelligence across music, clothing, basketball, food, community, and conversion.

## Public Site Metrics

Track:

- Page views.
- Unique visitors.
- World clicks.
- Boot sequence completion.
- Join da Symphony clicks.
- Social clicks.
- Shop clicks.
- YouTube clicks.
- Patreon clicks.
- Contact clicks.

## Content Metrics

Track:

- Top videos.
- Top world entries.
- Top CTAs.
- Click-through rate by world.
- Content that drives shop visits.
- Content that drives Patreon/community actions.

## Business Metrics

Future tracking:

- Product views.
- Drop signups.
- Email signups.
- Revenue.
- Conversion rate.
- Investor Mode views.
- Sponsor/collab inquiries.

## Dashboard Direction

Analytics should eventually live inside Harmony Core.

It should show:

- Today's performance.
- This week's performance.
- Top world.
- Top link.
- Top traffic source.
- Conversion events.

## Build Strategy

For v0.1, prepare events and structure.

Later connect to analytics tools such as Vercel Analytics, Google Analytics, PostHog, Plausible, or a custom database.
