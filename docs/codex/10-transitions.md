# Transition System

## Purpose

Transitions are part of the identity of Harmonic OS. They should make the site feel alive without becoming messy.

Every transition must have meaning.

## Core Transitions

### Boot Transition

Darkness to Cup to frequencies to Heart to OS.

### Cup Pour Transition

Used when content is being created, loaded, or refreshed.

### Frequency Wave Transition

Used when moving between worlds or sections.

### Heart Reveal

Used for emotional/core moments, brand reveal, or Harmonic Bible entries.

### Portal Open

Used to enter one of the Four Worlds.

### World Switch

Used when moving from Melodic to 2 Harmonic, Fried Em, or Schmackinn.

### Glass Panel Hover

Cards and panels should glow softly, lift slightly, and reveal depth.

### Mobile Tap Effects

Touch interactions should feel smooth and responsive, not tiny or desktop-like.

## Motion Rules

- Smooth over chaotic.
- Premium over flashy.
- Meaningful over random.
- Readable over over-animated.
- Use reduced motion preferences for accessibility.

## Animation Mood

The best animation should feel like frequency becoming interface.
