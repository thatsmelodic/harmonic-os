# Public Harmonic OS

## Purpose

The public OS is what visitors see first. It must quickly communicate that Melodic does not have a normal website. Visitors are entering a universe.

## v0.1 Required Experience

1. Boot screen.
2. Cup pour / frequency intro.
3. Harmonic Heart reveal.
4. `HARMONIC OS` title.
5. Tagline: `One Frequency. Many Worlds.`
6. Four World selection.
7. Join da Symphony social hub.
8. Harmonic Bible preview.
9. Footer/contact/social links.

## Four Worlds

Each world should be shown as a portal/card/panel.

### Melodic

Music, sound, creative identity.

CTAs:

- Listen.
- Watch.
- Follow.
- Join Patreon.

### 2 Harmonic

Fashion, Stitched Melodies, drops, brand philosophy.

CTAs:

- Shop.
- View collection.
- Follow 2 Harmonic.
- Read the meaning.

### Fried Em

Basketball content, episodes, smoke, competition, entertainment.

CTAs:

- Watch episodes.
- Follow.
- Challenge/submit.

### Schmackinn

Food reviews, taste tests, personality, culture.

CTAs:

- Watch reviews.
- Follow.
- Suggest food spots.

## Join da Symphony

This replaces Linktree inside Harmonic OS.

It should organize socials by world rather than dumping every link in one list.

Social categories:

- Melodic: Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, Patreon, Snapchat.
- 2 Harmonic: Instagram, website/shop.
- Fried Em: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram.
- Schmackinn: YouTube, TikTok, Instagram.
- Support: Cash App or donation/support links.

## Mobile

Mobile must feel like a premium app landing experience.

Avoid tiny desktop UI. Use large touch targets, vertical panels, smooth gestures, and simple navigation.
